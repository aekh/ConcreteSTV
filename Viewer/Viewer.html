Instructions for viewing election changes or transcripts

This page allows you to examine specific vote changes that can change outcomes in elections.

Click on the browse button above to select the contest. You will be shown one or more small vote changes or vote additions that alter the outcome in that contest, specifying:

  • Ballots: how many votes are involved
  • Effect: which candidate(s) are added or removed
  • How: whether the change requires vote changes or only additions, or restrictions to particular kinds of votes, such as ATL (above-the-line) or BTL (below-the-line)
  • Details: a summary of the vote changes. Hover your mouse over them to see exactly which votes are changed or added
