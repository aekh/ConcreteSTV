This site enables one to see the mean (average) preference given for each candidate, possibly restricted to below the line votes and/or votes with a particular candidate as the first preference.

Usage:

  • The box on the left of a name shows the mean vote.
  • Blanks are considered to be the mean of the unallocated preference numbers.
  • Only the formal part of formal votes are counted.
  • Click on a name to restrict to ballots with said candidate as first preference, or Clear.
